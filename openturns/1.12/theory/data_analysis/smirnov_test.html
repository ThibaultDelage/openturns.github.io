Smirnov samples comparison test¶

Let be a scalar uncertain variable modeled as a random variable. This method deals with the construction of a dataset prior to the choice of a probability distribution for . Smirnov’s test is a tool that may be used to compare two samples and ; the goal is to determine whether these two samples come from the same probability distribution or not. If this is the case, the two samples should be aggregated in order to increase the robustness of further statistical analysis.
